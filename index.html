About US

Specialist Supported Living (SSL Ltd) was borne of 10 years’ experience working at executive level with sector leading support providers, housing providers, developers and funders.

What we do

SSL Ltd collaborates directly with Support Providers, Housing Associations, Institutional funders, Local Authorities & CCG commissioning teams across the sector, ensuring best value for its partners in the delivery of outstanding, bespoke and sustainable solutions in:-

  • Business Development Strategy & Delivery

  • Disposal & Acquisition Portfolio Solutions

  • Service Transition Support - Deregistration to Supported Living

  • Service Level Contract/Agreement (SLC/SLA) advice and support

  • Supported Living Design & Specification

  • Project Finance

  • Development Capital

  • Housing Benefit
